# Refund policy

Paste this verbatim into FastSpring's refund-policy field on the application form, and onto the product page. Industry-standard for paid Mac apps; FastSpring rejects applications with vague or hostile refund terms.

---

**30-day money-back guarantee.**

If Gargantua doesn't work for you, email **support@inceptyon.com** within 30 days of purchase. No questions asked — we'll refund the full purchase price.

Your license remains valid until the refund is processed. After processing, the license deactivates on next launch.

Refunds are returned to the original payment method via FastSpring (our payment processor). Processing time is typically 3–5 business days.

**What we won't refund**: requests made more than 30 days after purchase. If you discover a problem outside that window, contact us anyway — we read every email and may make exceptions for genuine bugs.

**Source-built versions**: Gargantua is open source under AGPL-3.0. You can build and run a fully unlocked binary for free from https://github.com/inceptyon-labs/gargantua. If a refund seems easier than a purchase clarification, build from source while we sort it out.
